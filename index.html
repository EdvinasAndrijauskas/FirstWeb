Welcome to the Bike shop

We are now open our normal full hours - 8.30am to 7pm Monday to Friday - but for the time being we are still trying to avoid virus spreading so we are not letting more than one person in the shop at a time. To avoid too much interaction at the door, please book your bike in here and choose the slot when you want to drop it off. As you may know, bicycles are the new new toilet paper and we don't have any to sell. I would suggest About The Bike in Hackney for something cheap and functional and London Bicycle Workshop for good quality commuting bikes. Some of our old demo bikes are in these shops too. Don't forget everybody is struggling with supplies so don't give them a hard time!
